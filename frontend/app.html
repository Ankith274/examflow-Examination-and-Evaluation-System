⚙️ Process

How ExamFlow Works

Four simple steps from exam creation to results — everything automated.

1
Create Exam
Teacher adds questions, sets duration, and schedules for specific sections or the entire batch.
2
Students Login
Students log in with roll number. Camera + mic check. Exam begins with auto-shuffled questions.
3
AI Monitors Live
AI watches for violations in real-time. Teachers get instant alerts for high-severity events.
4
Instant Results
Auto-graded results published immediately. Export PDF marksheets. Full analytics available.
